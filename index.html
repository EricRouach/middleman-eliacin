Eliacin is a consultancy & investment company dedicated to Real Estate innovation, crossing its real estate experience with the most advanced trends in the PropTech field.
The PropTech domain is the transformation of the real estate market, thanks to new technologies and new players, offering innovative solutions changing the way the business used to be done.
Numerous start-ups address now the various pain points in this industry and investors are catching this drift as investment figures speak from themselves. Eliacin, through its dedicated vehicle, partners with a couple of promising ones (e.g. equity, corporate structuring, business development).
